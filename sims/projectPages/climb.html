Title: Full Stack Software Engineer

2019-2021 Home page

Climb is pretty cool, one of the many orgs looking at how to fund education that fits our strange modern world. When I was there, it was growing from a small startup (~20ppl) to a slightly less-small startup (last I checked, around 40ppl). Our team was small and we wore a lot of hats. Some tasks:

  • Correcting data entry errors, ensuring data cleanliness, etc.
  • Updating databases to fit new business needs (e.g. expanding to additional legal territories, educational categories)
  • General code maintenance, cleaning, refactoring
  • I added the annoying GDPR cookie thing when that dropped back in like 2019
  • Made a very cool 404 page (cat design by Rachel Seitz)
  • Taught a handful of Climb U classes (weekly knowledge-sharing sessions)
