No Rats Were Harmed

(in the Making of This Game)

No Rats Were Harmed is a 2024 collaboration with ILD Games' Nick Defossez. Made for the Boss Rush Game Jam '24, between January 13th and February 5th, we pushed our comfort zones and delivered an ambitious title combining 2D and 3D in 23 days. During that time, I personally created 6 pixel-art character concepts & animated full movesets for 4 of those characters. We integrated the 4 fully animated characters to deliver 3 boss fights.

Squatbot is a mobile platformer with a unique control scheme and an adorable robotic protagonist. As lead animator for squatbot, I created more than 60 different skins for the titular character, and a series of tools to help render full animation sets for each of those skins.
